Salar Jung Museum

The Salar Jung Museum is an art museum located at Dar-ul-Shifa, on the southern bank of the Musi River in the city of Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It is one of the notable National Museums of India.

How to Reach

The nearest airport to the Salarjung Museum is Hyderabad Airport and below are the directions from the airport to the museum.

  1. Drive from NH 44 to Gaganpahad
  2. Follow NH 44 to Salar Jung Rd
    1. Take a right turn
    2. Then next left and make a U turn at the kolhapur junction
    3. Then head down the lane towards the Darulshifa Highway bridge
  3. Follow Salar Jung Rd to your destination in Darulshifa.
